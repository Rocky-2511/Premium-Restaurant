Our Heritage

1998 - Present

A relentless pursuit of perfection.

What started as a small, intimate bistro in Paris has evolved into a globally recognized institution of fine dining. We spent decades refining our craft, traveling the world to understand how different cultures interact with food.

Every dish we serve is the culmination of years of testing, failing, and ultimately achieving balance.

The Mastermind

Chef Antoine Laurent

Trained under the rigorous demands of classic French kitchens, Chef Antoine brings discipline and wild creativity to L'Or. Awarded 3 Michelin Stars, his vision is the beating heart of our restaurant.
